About this Department

What is the Intelligent Mechatronics Engineering?

  • Introduction

    The meaning of Intelligent Mechatronics Engineering is a combinational technology of artificial intelligence, mechanics, electronics, and engineering.
    Students learn the most important core technologies in the 4th Industrial Revolution,
    which include combining artificial intelligence technologies with machinery and electronic systems,
    such as smart cars, drones, robots, home appliances, IoT, and semiconductors.

  • Educational Goals

    1. Training of human resources with creative intelligent system development and problem-solving skills
    2. Training of competitive human resources with professional practical skills required by industrial fields
    3. Training of global talent with communication and humanities
    4. Training of future technology convergence and collaboration skills
